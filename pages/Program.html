BBL Fine Arts Academy



The BBL Fine Arts Academy provides small group lessons and ensemble rehearsals to supplement the local middle and high school band programs.

We currently offer lessons for voice, brass (trumpet, trombone), reeds (clarinet, alto saxophone, tenor saxophone, baritone saxophone), and percussion (drums, piano, guitar, bass guitar)

*Financial scholarships available on a first come, first served basis and dependent on need as determined by free or reduced public school lunch status

DETAILS OF INSTRUCTION:

Monday-Thursday

Individual and small group instruction beginners through advanced. Instruction includes one 30 minute lesson per week for beginning/intermediate students, and a 30-50 minute session for advanced students. ENSEMBLE INSTRUCTION - SATURDAYS


Beginners 11:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.


- Students in this program recently started playing their instruments; generally, these are fifth grade students but occasionally MS & HS students fall in this category depending on their experience

Intermediate Band (2-3 years experience) 12:00 p.m.-1:30 p.m.


- Generally, this ensemble is composed of middle school students and high school students. Students in this group learn how to improvise and develop that skill to participate in the Advanced ensemble.

Advanced small ensemble (3 plus years) 1:30 p.m.-2:30 p.m.


- Students in this group are high school aged and sometimes are in middle school. Members of this ensemble are familiar with and can execute the blues scale, modal scales, music theory related to improvisation and much more. Membership of this ensemble is based on audition, director discretion and skill level.
